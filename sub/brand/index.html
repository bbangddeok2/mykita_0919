THE STORY OF A NAME

Founded in Berlin in 2003,
the first MYKITA premises were settled
in a former children’s day care,
called Kita in German.

This inspired not only the name My Kita,
but also our culture of curiosity and play.

We started with a clear aesthetic vision
and a readiness to do things ourselves.

Creating an open-minded work environment
and a sense of integrity toward everything in
and around the product came intuitively.
Founded in Berlin in 2003,
MYKITA’s name reflects
curiosity and play.

We began with a clear vision
and hands-on spirit.
Founded in 2003,
MYKITA embodies curiosity and play.

INDEPENDENT MANUFACTURING

MYKITA started out in the same independent
and pioneering spirit that shaped our hometown, Berlin.
With no one to manufacture our original eyewear designs,
the decision was made to set up the in-house production.

Developing our own tools and processes
to meet design requirements has since become
a principle of how MYKITA operates
and one of our greatest assets.
Born from Berlin’s independent spirit,
MYKITA built its own production.
Craftsmanship meets technology
in every frame.
MYKITA HAUS drives innovation and quality.

EVERYTHING UNDER ONE ROOFE

In 2014, MYKITA settled into a historic building
in the heart of Berlin Kreuzberg.

Headed by founder Moritz Krueger,
the team at MYKITA HAUS is a mix of people with
diverse cultural backgrounds and individual talents
who guide the product through every stage.
Since 2014, MYKITA HAUS
in Kreuzberg has been
home to a diverse team.
Together they drive
innovation and quality.
MYKITA HAUS drives innovation and quality.
